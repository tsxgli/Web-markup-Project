Bora Bora

This is a Prench Polynesian island. It is known for its tropical nature and its blue waters. This island is located Northwest of Tahiti and the airport is located on another part called Motu Mete. To get to the island you travel by boat. The islands mountains are very good for hiking and also for taking photos and so if you are interested in those, this is a great choice for a vacation. As for swimming, Bora Bora's waters are a great place to swim and also to relax. It also looks very good in photos. Book Bora Bora for your vacation and you won't regret it !

Santorini

Santorini is known for its white historic looking buildings and its view of the ocean. This place looks different than other vacation spots, mainly because it has no tropics. But those things are what makes Santorini special compared most vacation spots. Santorini is located in the southern Aegan sea, one of the Cyclade islands and its considered one of the most beautiful greek islands. Its also a perfect honeymoon vacation place because of the good sunny weather and the other places to go.
